Here's one of my favorite recipes:

Vegetarian Chickpea & Quinoa Salad

  • Step 1
    Chop:
    1/4 onion
    1 red bell pepper
    4 celery stalks
    Mix in 2 cans of Chickpeas
  • Step 2
    Add:
    4 tbsp. of Lemon juice
    1 tbsp. Olive Oil
    Pinch of Salt
  • Step 3
    Add:
    1/2 cup of cooked quinoa
    Mix everything together

    ENJOY!
